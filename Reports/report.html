Aim

The overall aim of this entire assignment is to create a short Film with a transformer.
In this part of the assignment we rendered the transformer, made the vehicle drivable and added advanced lighting and camera motion

Driving

The transformer moves on the surface of the environment we created in both the vehicle as well as the humanoid state.
Moves forward, backward, left and right using the respective arrow keys.
The wheels rotate respective to the body to a maximum of 45 degrees in either direction.

Camera

There are three cameras

  1. Global
    This is a camera giving a bird's eyes view of the scene
  2. Third person camera
    This is a camera just behind the robot/tank, that follows it around the terrain
  3. First person camera
    This is a camera giving a perspective view as seen by the robot/tank

Lighting

Lighting is of the following forms:

  1. Directed
    These are two directed lights, activate by the Home or Delete keys. One provides lighting to the Bird's eye view. The other provides lighting to the other cameras
  2. Headlights
    These are spotlights, attached to the car, activated by F9
